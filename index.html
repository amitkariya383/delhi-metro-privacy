Privacy Policy - Delhi Metro Planner

Last updated: June 2025

Delhi Metro Planner respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information Collection

We only collect information necessary for the app's functionality, such as your search history and favorite stations. This data is stored locally on your device.

Use of Data

  • To provide route planning services
  • To improve app experience
  • To remember your recent searches

Advertisements

This app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Ad providers may use your device information and usage data to show relevant ads.

Google AdMob may collect the following data:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Location information (approximate)

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — Advertising service
  • Google Play Services — App distribution and updates

Data Security

Your data is securely stored on your device. We do not share your personal information with any third parties, except for the advertising services mentioned above.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Cookies

We use cookies only to remember your language preference and theme settings.

Disclaimer

This is not an official app of Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC). This is an independent app built using publicly available information. For accurate information, please refer to the official DMRC website.

Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
